People Systems

Strengthening the HR systems that enable scale, consistency, and experience

As organisations grow, their people practices must evolve in step. Scaling places increasing demands on HR to provide clarity, consistency, and business relevance, while supporting managers and leaders with systems that are fair, reliable, and scalable.

People systems that worked well at smaller scale often start to strain as complexity increases. Performance processes become uneven, recruitment decisions vary widely by manager, reward structures lose their line of sight to contribution, and employee experience becomes inconsistent across teams and levels.

Our People Systems work focuses on strengthening the core HR-owned systems that sit underneath everyday people decisions, ensuring they are aligned to business priorities, well governed, and capable of supporting growth.

CAPABILITIES

What We Focus On

HR Foundations & Operating Effectiveness

We work with HR leaders to strengthen the foundations required to operate confidently at scale, clarifying operating models, roles, and routines. This involves refining partnering models, decision rights, governance, and escalation mechanisms.

Performance Management

We help redesign performance approaches, so they are aligned to strategy, simple to operate, and meaningful for managers and employees. Focus areas include philosophy, goal alignment, reviews, calibration, and dialogue coaching.

Rewards & Recognition

We help organisations build business-relevant and manageable reward structures that reinforce contribution and performance, establishing incentive plans and variable pay structures aligned to specific roles.

Recruitment & Selection Systems

We help strengthen recruitment and selection systems by bringing greater structure and consistency to how roles are defined, candidates are assessed, and early tenure onboarding processes are governed.

Employee Value Proposition (EVP)

We help organisations define and activate an experience-led EVP that is grounded in reality, reflecting actual employee experience, and integrated cleanly with leadership standard practices.

Employee Experience & Culture

Culture is reinforced through systems and routines. We help align performance, rewards, and people processes to ensure that cultural intent is experienced consistently across all teams.
