OVERVIEW

As a business scales, its leadership has to scale with it.

Growth places new demands on leaders and teams. The capability, succession depth, and alignment that worked at one size rarely carry unchanged to the next.

We assess and develop leaders against where the business is going, not where it has been.

PROPRIETARY METHODOLOGY

The Leader Essence© Method

A deeper read on whether a leader is right for what’s next.

DKOA’s proprietary leadership assessment and development methodology. Most assessments stop at what a leader has done. Leader Essence reads that track record, and the layer beneath it: who the leader fundamentally is, and what that predicts about how they will perform as the role grows.

What it looks at

Whether a proven track record will hold at greater scale and complexity
How a leader operates under pressure, not just on paper
What drives them, and whether that fits what the role demands
Their underlying values and belief system, and how those shape leadership
How they adapt and grow, and where they are most likely to derail

What it delivers

A clear, evidence-based read on whether a leader is right for the role ahead: their strengths, their risks, and the development that will matter most. Confidence in hiring, promotion, succession, and transition decisions that carry real consequence.

Read within the realities of growth and complexity, not against a generic leadership model.
